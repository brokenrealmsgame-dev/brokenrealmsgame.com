Core Features

Physics-Based Combat

Swing harder, hit harder. Every weapon, every spell, every punch scales with your real physical effort.

Fireballs & Magic Guns

Conjure fireballs from your palms. Throw them, slam them together, or coat your sword in flames.

Roguelite Progression

Loot weapons, unlock talents, grow stronger. Die, learn, repeat. Each run makes you more powerful.

Play Your Way

Standing or seated — switch anytime. No bending required. Designed so everyone can play.

About the Game

Grab a fireball from thin air. Hurl it at a charging wolf. Draw your sword and cleave through the pack. Fire your magic gun from range. Slam two fireballs together for a massive explosion. Coat your blade in flames. Punch an incoming projectile back at the archer who fired it.

Five procedurally generated levels. Champion enemies with deadly abilities. Epic boss fights. Loot with five rarity tiers. Talent trees. Hardcore permadeath mode.

This is Broken Realms.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2025

Overview

Broken Realms VR ("the Game") is a single-player VR game developed by Broken Realms Studio ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about what data we do and do not collect.

Data We Collect

The Game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no online features in the current version.

Steam Platform

The Game is distributed through Steam. Valve Corporation may collect data through the Steam platform in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not have access to your personal Steam data. Please review Steam's Privacy Policy for details.

Website

This website (brokenrealms.com) is a static site. We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. External links to third-party services (Discord, YouTube, TikTok, X) are subject to their respective privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

We maintain community channels on the following platforms. Your use of these platforms is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Discord
  • YouTube
  • TikTok
  • X (formerly Twitter)

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

As the Game evolves (including potential future multiplayer features), this policy will be updated accordingly. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our Discord server.

THE GAME IN ONE LINE:
A physics-based VR action RPG where you swing swords, hurl fireballs, brew
potions, and battle through 5 procedurally generated levels of escalating
danger — looting your way stronger with every kill.

THE SOUL OF THE GAME:
"I am flying, and I have a fireball in my hand."
Every design decision serves this feeling. Playtesters said they could
"feel the love" in this game.

================================================================================
SECTION 1: THE GOAL
================================================================================

WHAT THE PLAYER DOES:
You are a hero in a fantasy VR world. You fight through 5 increasingly
dangerous levels, killing enemies to gain XP, collecting loot to grow
stronger, and unlocking new abilities — until you face the final boss.

THE LOOP:
1. CREATE your character (name them, pick a class)
2. ENTER the first level — a procedurally generated fantasy landscape
3. FIGHT enemy camps scattered across the map
4. LOOT weapons, potions, treasure, and crafting ingredients from fallen foes
5. COMPLETE quests from NPCs ("Slay 10 wolves", "Collect 5 tiger fangs")
6. SHOP at vendors for better weapons using gold earned from treasure
7. CRAFT potions at cauldrons by physically throwing flowers into them
8. FIND THE PORTAL to advance to the next, harder level
9. GROW STRONGER — level up, find rare weapons, unlock talents
10. FACE THE BOSS — a multi-phase monstrosity on Level 5
11. SURVIVE — return to character select, start a new run with your progress

THE FEELING:
Each level is harder. Enemies hit harder. But YOU are also stronger — your
Level 1 common sword is now a Level 8 Legendary fire-enchanted greatsword
with +30% crit chance. The fireball that tickled wolves now obliterates
entire packs. You started cautious. Now you're a force of nature.

PERMADEATH OPTION (HARDCORE MODE):
Create a Hardcore character. Death is permanent — your character is deleted.
Every fight matters. Every health potion is precious. Marked with [HC] in
the character list so everyone knows you earned it.

SECTION 3: COMBAT SYSTEM
================================================================================

ALL COMBAT IS PHYSICS-BASED. Velocity = power. Everywhere. Always.

--- MELEE WEAPONS ---
Swords, Axes, Maces, Daggers, Staves
- Physically swing with your VR controllers
- Harder swing = more damage (physics-based velocity scaling)
- Sphere trace hit detection from hilt to tip
- Knockback force scales with swing velocity
- Status effects proc from weapon bonuses (fire, cold, poison, lightning)
- Base sword damage: 50
- Hit cooldown per enemy: 0.2s (prevents wiggle-spam)

--- RANGED WEAPONS (GUNS) ---
Fireball Guns, Frost Guns, etc.
- Trigger to fire, hold for auto-fire
- Auto-fire rate: 0.1s between shots
- Max ammo: 40 rounds
- Projectile damage: 30 base
- Muzzle flash + projectile VFX
- Ammo count displayed on weapon

--- MAGIC ---
- FIREBALL THROWING: Grab a fireball from your hand and physically throw it
- SLAM FUSION: Smash two fireballs together = combined explosion
- DRAW APART: Pull hands 60cm+ apart fast = massive 2x size explosion
- THROW LOAD: Wind up behind head = powered-up throw
- FIRE COATING: Hold sword near fireball for 1 second = 60s fire enchant (+50 fire damage)
- PUNCH DEFLECT: Punch a flying projectile to redirect it (2x velocity)

--- SHIELDS ---
- Block incoming projectiles and melee attacks
- Shield bash for knockback (2000 force, 15 base damage)
- Ricochet enemy projectiles back at them

--- FIST COMBAT ---
- Punch enemies bare-handed with physics-based damage
- Buff-enhanced fist damage and knockback

--- STATUS EFFECTS ON ENEMIES ---
| Effect    | Trigger    | What It Does                              | Duration   |
|-----------|------------|-------------------------------------------|------------|
| BURNING   | 10% chance | Fire DoT (10% of fire bonus per 3s tick)  | 30 seconds |
| COLD SLOW | 25% chance | Halves enemy movement speed               | 10 seconds |
| POISONED  | 10% chance | Stacking poison ticks (max 3 stacks)      | 60 seconds |
| RUPTURE   | Every hit  | Burst at 20% maxHP threshold for bonus dmg | Decays 5/s |

--- DAMAGE TYPES ---
5 types: Physical, Fire, Cold, Poison, Lightning
Mixed damage possible (e.g. flaming sword = Physical 50 + Fire 25)
Enemies have Armor (reduces Physical) and Magic Armor (reduces all magical)

SECTION 5: WEAPON RARITY SYSTEM
================================================================================

5-tier rarity system rolled when weapons drop:

| Rank | Name      | Color  | Random Bonuses | Drop Weight |
|------|-----------|--------|----------------|-------------|
| 1    | Common    | Gray   | 1 bonus        | 50%         |
| 2    | Uncommon  | Green  | 2 bonuses      | 30%         |
| 3    | Rare      | Blue   | 3 bonuses      | 15%         |
| 4    | Epic      | Purple | 4 bonuses      | 4%          |
| 5    | Legendary | Gold   | 5 bonuses      | 1%          |

Higher difficulty shifts weights toward rarer drops.

--- WEAPON LEVEL ---
Damage multiplier = 1.15^(level-1) — compound 15% per level.
Level 1 = 1.0x, Level 5 = 1.75x, Level 10 = 3.52x

--- POSSIBLE STAT BONUSES ---
- +% Damage
- +Flat Fire / Cold / Poison / Lightning Damage
- +Ammo Regen per second (guns only)
- -Knockback Cooldown (swords + shields)
- +% Attack Speed (swords + guns)
- +% Life Steal (swords + guns)
- +% Crit Chance (all weapons)

--- DPS COMPARISON ---
When inspecting a weapon, DPS is calculated and compared vs. equipped:
  Green + = better than what you have
  Yellow = = roughly equal
  Red -   = worse than what you have

--- ELEMENTAL ENCHANTMENT FLAMES ---
Weapons with high elemental bonuses get visible flame VFX:
- Fire bonus → orange/red flames along blade
- Cold bonus → blue/white frost flames
- Poison bonus → green toxic flames
- Lightning bonus → purple/white electric arcs

SECTION 6: LOOT SYSTEM
================================================================================

--- LOOT DROPS ---
Enemies explode into loot on death with a "fountain effect":
- Items pop upward with staggered timing (cascading visual)
- Float briefly with disabled physics
- Physics activates and items fall naturally
- All items auto-levitate to grab height (NO BENDING OVER)

--- ITEM TYPES ---
- Weapons (swords, axes, maces, daggers, staves)
- Guns (fireball, frostball, etc.)
- Shields
- Consumables (potions, food, flowers)
- Treasure (auto-collect gold on touch)
- Quest Items (tracked by quest givers)
- Buff Chests (pick-one-of-three buff upgrade)

--- LAZY INJECTION (Performance) ---
Items on the ground are just a mesh + overlap sphere (very lightweight).
When grabbed, the appropriate components (SwordComponent, GunComponent,
ConsumableComponent, etc.) are dynamically injected. This means 200+ items
on the ground cost almost nothing.

--- ITEM ECONOMY ---

|---------------|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| HP Potion     | Heal 50 HP                | 70%       | 25 gold    |
| Mana Potion   | Restore 30 Mana           | 55%       | 30 gold    |
| Recall Potion | Teleport to starting tower | 25%       | 50 gold    |

SECTION 9: BOSS FIGHT (LEVEL 5)
================================================================================

THE FINAL BOSS — "Dimension Guardian"
HP: 1000 (10x base), Damage: 45 (3x base), Scale: 2x normal size

--- 3 PHASES ---
| Phase | Triggers At  | Behavior                                   |
|-------|-------------|---------------------------------------------|
| 0     | Fight start | Full spell rotation, can be stunned/slowed   |
| 1     | 66% HP      | Boss flees, transforms, unstunnable          |
| 2     | 33% HP      | Desperate phase, all spells, shorter cooldowns|

--- 6 BOSS SPELL TYPES ---
1. SUMMON MINIONS (near self): 3-5 aggressive minions spawn around boss
2. SUMMON MINIONS (near player): 3-4 minions appear between you and the boss
3. AOE GROUND TELEGRAPH: Red circle pulses on ground, explodes after 3s warning
   (500-600cm radius, 80-100 damage — DODGE!)
4. SINGLE PROJECTILE: Fires at player
5. GROUND STAMP: Close-range AoE at boss's feet
6. PROJECTILE BARRAGE: 5-7 projectiles with 30-45 degree spread, optional homing

Phase transitions: boss flees to a distance, visual transformation plays,
then returns with new abilities and immunity to stuns.

SECTION 10: VENDORS & ECONOMY
================================================================================

Vendor NPCs appear on Levels 1 and 3 (both biome zones).

--- BUYING ---
- 6 randomly-rolled weapons displayed on vendor stall
- Each weapon has random level + rarity
- Price scales with weapon power (BuyPriceMultiplier x sell value)
- PHYSICALLY GRAB a weapon to buy it
- Can't afford it? It snaps back to the display

--- SELLING ---
- Drop items onto the vendor's SELL BOX
- Non-weapon items convert to gold (treasure dust)
- Weapons/guns/shields cannot be sold (too valuable!)
- Holstered items are rejected (must physically drop into box)

--- DUST ECONOMY ---
5 currency types: Treasure (gold), Botanical, Might, Swift, Legendary
- Treasure = primary gold for buying
- Other types feed talent trees
- Items can be burned in furnace for dust of their configured type

SECTION 12: SAVE SYSTEM
================================================================================

--- PER-CHARACTER PERSISTENCE ---
- Player stats (level, XP, difficulty, playtime, total kills)
- Left + Right holster weapons (with FULL rarity bonuses preserved)
- 25-slot backpack inventory (5x5 grid)
- Talent tree purchases
- Dust wallet (all 5 types)
- Weapon XP per weapon type
- Milestone achievements

--- AUTO-SAVE TRIGGERS ---
- Level up, Talent purchase, Portal transition, Item changes (30s coalesce), Game quit

--- MODES ---
- NORMAL: Full persistence across runs
- HARDCORE: Death = character permanently deleted
- DEMO MODE: Everything works but nothing saves to disk (kiosk/convention mode)

SECTION 17: PERFORMANCE
================================================================================

- WiFi VR: 110-120 FPS STABLE (confirmed)
- Editor: ~60 FPS (expected without stereo instanced rendering)
- Map generation: 340ms (optimized from 1598ms)
- Custom ECS-style mass enemy system (not UE5 Mass Entity)
  - Actor pool with LOD-based tick rates
  - LOD0 = every frame, LOD1 = every 8th frame
  - Frustum culling, distance-based collision
- Loot optimization:
  - Lazy component injection (items on ground = mesh only)
  - Distance-based rotation skip (>5m = no spinning)
  - Tick disabled after 30s on ground
- ISM→HISM converter for vegetation performance

SECTION 18: KEY DIFFERENTIATORS (Why This Game Stands Out)
================================================================================

1. PHYSICS = POWER: Swing harder = more damage. Consistent across ALL weapons.
   Not just a gimmick — it's the core mechanic.

2. DEEP RPG IN VR: 5 talent trees, 5-tier weapon rarity, 5 dust currencies,
   20-character roster, milestone achievements. This depth is rare in VR.

3. ACCESSIBILITY FIRST: No bending, magic grab, toggle grip, wrist UI.
   Designed so elderly, disabled, and first-time VR players can enjoy it.
   Everyone wins, skill is rewarded.

4. ROGUELIKE RUNS: Each run through 5 levels is different. Procedural maps,
   random loot, random vendors, random cauldrons. But your character persists.

5. PHYSICAL CRAFTING: Throw flowers into a cauldron. Watch them brew.
   Grab the potion that launches out. It feels REAL.

6. HARDCORE PERMADEATH: For players who want real stakes.

7. PHYSICS MAGIC COMBOS: Slam two fireballs together. Draw them apart for
   bigger explosions. Coat your sword in fire. Punch projectiles back.

8. SOLO DEV PASSION: "Feel the love" — handcrafted with care.
